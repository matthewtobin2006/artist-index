About
Artist Index

Artist Index is a personal music reference — a curated, editorially designed record of the artists who have defined their eras from the 2020s back through the decades.

Each artist profile pulls live data from the iTunes Search API for real album artwork, discographies, and track listings. The Billboard page updates weekly with the current Hot 100's most active artists.

Browse by decade. Click any artist to open their full profile — bio, origin, label, complete discography, and their ten most iconic recordings.

Built as a companion to the Stadium Index project. Same editorial sensibility, different obsession.
